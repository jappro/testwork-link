ABOUT VINCESLINK

Our Story

Vinceslink was founded with a vision to simplify modern living by bringing together three essential aspects of daily life: technology, everyday essentials, and property solutions. What started as a passion for providing quality gadgets has evolved into a multi-industry brand committed to excellence and customer satisfaction.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional value through quality products and reliable services while maintaining transparency, trust, and convenience in every customer interaction. We strive to be the go-to platform where technology meets lifestyle and opportunity meets reality.

Our Vision

To become Nigeria's leading multi-industry ecosystem, where customers can access authentic gadgets, essential household items, and trusted real estate solutions‚Äîall under one trusted brand.

Vincent

Founder & CEO

FAQ

How do I place an order? +
Currently, you can contact us directly via WhatsApp, phone, or visit our physical stores. Our e-commerce platform is coming soon for seamless online ordering.
What are your delivery options? +
We offer delivery services within Ogun State and surrounding areas. Contact us for specific delivery times and charges based on your location.
How does the property consultation work? +
Fill out our consultation form with your property interests and budget. Our real estate team will contact you within 24-48 hours to schedule a viewing or discuss available options.

We accept returns within 7 days for defective products. Items must be in original condition with all packaging. Contact our support team to initiate a return.
