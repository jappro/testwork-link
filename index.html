ABOUT VINCESLINK

Our Story

Vinceslink was founded with a vision to simplify modern living by bringing together three essential aspects of daily life: technology, everyday essentials, and property solutions. What started as a passion for providing quality gadgets has evolved into a multi-industry brand committed to excellence and customer satisfaction.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional value through quality products and reliable services while maintaining transparency, trust, and convenience in every customer interaction. We strive to be the go-to platform where technology meets lifestyle and opportunity meets reality.

FAQ

Have questions? We've got answers! Click below to view all frequently asked questions.

How do I place an order? +
Once our e-commerce platform launches, you'll be able to browse products, add them to your cart, and complete your purchase securely. You can also contact us directly via WhatsApp or phone for immediate assistance.
What are your delivery areas? +
We currently serve Ogun State and surrounding areas. For gadgets and super store products, we offer pickup and delivery options. Contact us for specific delivery information to your location.
How does the property consultation process work? +
Simply fill out our consultation form with your requirements, and our real estate team will contact you within 24 hours to discuss available properties, schedule viewings, and guide you through the entire process.
What is your refund policy? +
We offer refunds for defective products within 7 days of purchase with proof of purchase. Items must be in original condition with packaging. Contact our support team to initiate a refund request.
What are your support hours? +
Our customer support is available Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM. You can reach us via phone, email, or WhatsApp. We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours.
